• Mission

      To seed children's imagination, cultivate their creativity, and grow mutual empathy through the arts for a more prosperous and peaceful future.

      Vision

      To motivate children to become "Creative-Empaths"—a sterling human character and quality which mirrors that of the Founding Fathers.

    TABATHA Araneo, 12

    “My work represents the inequality of access to resources in the world. I sought to reflect how a few have access to much and how many have access to nothing. At the same time, for those few, the fact of being privileged by an unjust social system represents a latent threat, which is represented by the hands that lurk from the confines of the work.”

    What We Do
    ICAF inspires students to boldly step beyond the boundaries set before them by envisioning a creative-empathic world where diversity and differences enhance the fractal of human discovery and evolution.
    • Arts Olympiad
      An art and sports program that fosters creativity and reduces obesity.
    • World Children's Festival
      A transformative experience that offsets Adverse Childhood Experiences and severs the intergenerational transmission of trauma and hatred.
    • Interactive Exhibition
      Creative activities to facilitate collaborative innovation by kindling the inner child of business executives.
    • ChildArt Magazine
      An ad-free quarterly that inspires learners of all ages and grows global competencies.
